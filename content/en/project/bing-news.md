# Bing News

> Help people break their information cocoons
> 

Bing News was the first project I took over after joining Microsoft. It is an essential part of the Bing search engine. When users perform searches, all news-related modules and links are provided by our team.

Although the search engine is a mature product, delivering quality news within it requires specialized expertise. Therefore, my daily work involves continuously optimizing and iterating on Bing News, with goals including achieving better ***Business growth (DAU)*** and ***User experience***.

### Business Growth

![The iteration process of Bing News on mobile end](/assets/images/bing-news/bing-news-Untitled.png)

**Before - Mar 2023**

Added "Top News" and "Explore more news" which overall increased the DAU of the news module.

**Mar 2023 - Aug 2023**

Changed the card swiping to a list view, making the reading experience more natural for users. Expanded the area for top news to further increase the DAU of the news module. Balanced the visual weight of each news item within the module.

**Aug 2023 - Now (2024)**

Due to business growth pressure, I changed the news title color from black to "Blue Link" and moved the publisher logo before the news title. Added small image previews to ‚ÄúExplroe more news‚Äù, further increasing the DAU of the news module.

### Trending on Bing

![Trending on Bing in Bing homepage](/assets/images/bing-news/bing-news-Untitled_3.png)

I noted that in the China, Japan, and Korea, users are more sensitive to text-based news, whereas in Western markets, users are more responsive to images. I believe this difference is due to the varying information density and text readability in different languages. Therefore, I designed different versions of the user interface accordingly.

### How I lock down a good design treatment?

All feature shipments of Bing require a flight test first. The test results must show comprehensive improvements over the previous version before shipping online. As a result, the design team need to put huge efforts on design exploration, striving to find the optimal design solution to meet the stringent shipping requirements.

Fortunately, we can leverage the immense traffic of the search engine to conduct extensive A/B testing. Additionally, Microsoft provides numerous mature professional tools to help us evaluate the effectiveness of design solutions. This allows me to efficiently measure and optimize the user experience, ensuring that every feature released achieves the best possible outcome.

![AB Test example](/assets/images/bing-news/bing-news-Untitled_5.png)
